India has 15107 Km of land border, and a coastline of 7517 Km with 197 islands accounting for 2094 Km of additional coastline. These borders comprise a vast variety of terrain encompassing deserts, plains, riverine deltas, hills, mountains, high altitude, and glaciated regions. According to the Ministry of Tourism, over 6.19 million and 1.52 million foreign tourists arrived in India in 2022 and 2021.

In this website, you get to see places from most famous Shimla Valley to least explored North East, to the unexplored, uninfluenced natural hotspots of Lakshadweep, to the back waters of Kerala and surreal beauty of nilgiris.....what not, you have everything in India.
